    Our Service

    We provide white label managed application service to mobile operators and content providers.

    Service to Mobile Operator

    eAppair team up with mobile operators to design and deploy service to increase customer loyalty by providing vertical solution to its business customers We are a provider of fully managed white label service. Services are designed to be scalable and suitable for carrier wide deployment. We understand the requirement for compliance and regulatory guidelines.

    Through our international partners with extensive experience in emerging market we can help operators lower the cost and speed up the deployment while ensuring that the service remains reliable and useful.

    Service to operator can be for
    • Internal usage
    • White label service for enterprise customers
    • Marketplace service to SME

    Service to Content Provider

    eAppair provides turkey deployment of SMS and IVR service to Content Providers, so they can concentrate on creating content. We have experienced technical resource for System Integration with all operators in Bangladesh.

    eAppair is not a content provider in traditional sense, thus free ourselves from any conflicting interest with the CP Service. Also in policy eAppair does not disclose or promote any service referring to client list, which re-enforce our commitment beyond NDA.

    Service to content provider can be form
    • SMS Gateway Integration
    • IVR Integration
    • Co-ordination during deployment with operators
    • Connectivity providers.
    • Billing system integration
